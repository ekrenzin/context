## When to Use

Use this skill whenever a significant decision is made within a project. This ensures that all decisions are documented consistently and can be referenced in the future.

## Workflow

1. **Identify Decision**: Clearly define the decision that has been made.
2. **Capture Context**: Document the context in which the decision was made, including any relevant background information.
3. **Reasoning**: Record the reasoning behind the decision, including any alternatives considered and why they were not chosen.
4. **Impact Assessment**: Evaluate and note the potential impact of the decision on the project.
5. **Approval**: Ensure the decision is reviewed and approved by relevant stakeholders.
6. **Documentation**: Enter the decision and its details into the project decision log.
7. **Communication**: Share the documented decision with the project team and any other relevant parties.

## Validation

- Verify that all sections of the decision documentation are complete and clear.
- Ensure that the decision has been approved by the necessary stakeholders.
- Confirm that the decision has been communicated to all relevant parties.
